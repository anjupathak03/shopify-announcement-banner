# 2-3 Minute Demo Script

1. Show the Shopify Admin app dashboard.
2. Type: `Sale 50% Off`.
3. Click **Save** and show the success message.
4. Open MongoDB and show the newest `announcements` document.
5. Open the theme editor and confirm the app embed is enabled.
6. Open Online Store preview and show the banner.
7. Close with: "The important part is that the storefront reads from Shopify's shop metafield through Liquid, not from my database or a deprecated ScriptTag."
